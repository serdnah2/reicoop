1
Code Your Future: Construye Paz
In partnership with Alcaldía de Bogotá and Unidad, we provided tech training, inspirational activities and employability opportunities for victims of the internal armed conflict in Colombia. We have impacted 130 people in Colombia's main cities (Bogotá, Barranquilla, Cali and Medellín).
2
New Generations (SENA)
We leverage a mandatory policy from Colombian Gov, into a opportunity to develop IT talent new generations from low income communities in the country. We’ve benefited 482 young people since 2018.
3
The Inspire Garage
Our main program to inspire and connect children and youth with technology, we have carried out several initiatives like Alianza Futuro Digital (60), school competitions with Minecraft (3 school teams in Medellín in the final), Tech Talks with Crack The Code (128) and regular Inspire Garages activities like workshops, talks, garages (3.493).
4
CYF Scholarships
We have provided 3.137 scholarships in a variety of education in technology programs to Colombian people.
5
Eduencers
Our local program that seeks to empower teachers and professors in IT related fields in technical institutions.
6
Be Kind Campaigns
Blood Donation, Back to School, Giving Back in the Holidays.
