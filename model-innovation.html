Georgia Tech Medellin Center (GTMC) for Innovation and Entrepreneurship

1
Objective

Strengthen the local innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem, translating into:

  1. Greater density of fast-growing Tech Startups (more startups and better quality).
  2. More ecosystem actors trained and certified by GTMC in innovation, entrepreneurship, and technology.
  3. Greater collaboration between universities, corporations, and startups.
  4. More startups created in local universities by students and faculty.
2
Goals

Ecosystem

Startup Ecosystem and Startups + Corporates

  1. 25 professors trained in the I-Corps Basic Training.
  2. 500 students enrolled in the Create-X program and local capacity was installed.
  3. 100 people trained in an entrepreneurship program.

Talent Development

  1. 1000 trained and placed in companies.

Innovation from Universities

  1. 50 projects TRL 0-4 scaled to TRL 6-7.
  2. University patent portfolio evaluation ready for commercialization up to 100.
